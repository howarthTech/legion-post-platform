Last updated: July 7, 2026

These terms govern the event-reminder text-message program ("the Program") operated by Howarth Tech Solutions on behalf of American Legion posts that use Legion Post Websites. By opting in you agree to these terms.

Program: American Legion post event & meeting reminders.
Message frequency: varies — typically a few messages per month.
Message and data rates may apply.

How you opt in

You are added to a post's reminder list only when you ask that post to add you. When a post officer adds you, you receive a single confirmation text asking you to reply YES. You will not receive any reminders unless you reply YES. This is your express consent to receive recurring informational text messages from your post.

How to get help or opt out

  • Reply HELP at any time for help, or contact support@legionpostwebsites.com.
  • Reply STOP at any time to cancel. You will receive one confirmation that you have been unsubscribed, and no further messages will be sent unless you opt in again.

What we send

Messages are strictly informational — reminders about your post's meetings and events. We do not send marketing or promotional messages through this Program.

Privacy
